Privacy Policy

This policy explains how website enquiries and submitted information may be handled through TheBrandHouse digital platforms.

Information you may provide

Visitors may provide personal information when using the contact page, careers contact details or backoffice login area. This may include names, email addresses, phone numbers, company information and resume details.

How information may be used

  • To respond to enquiries and follow up on requests sent through the website.
  • To review job-related applications and future vacancy interest.
  • To maintain website administration and authorised backoffice access.
  • To improve the clarity, performance and usefulness of the website.

Data sharing

TheBrandHouse does not use this website to publicly disclose personal information submitted by visitors. Information may be shared internally where necessary to process an enquiry, recruitment interest or support request.

Retention and protection

Reasonable steps should be taken to protect submitted information. Data may be retained for operational, recruitment or administrative follow-up where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact info@thebrandhouse.mu.
