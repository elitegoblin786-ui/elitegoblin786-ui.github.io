Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What products does TheBrandHouse offer?

+

TheBrandHouse offers a wide range of home appliances and consumer electronics, including refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, televisions, kitchen appliances, and more from trusted international brands.

How long does warranty cover?

+

Warranty coverage varies by product and brand. Typically, appliances come with 1-3 years of manufacturer warranty. Our after-sales service team can provide specific details for each product.

Do you offer delivery and installation?

+

Yes, we offer professional delivery and installation services for most products. Free delivery is available for purchases above a certain amount. Contact your local Galaxy store for specific details.

What are your store hours?

+

Store hours vary by location. Most Galaxy stores are open Monday-Saturday from 10 AM to 7 PM, and Sunday from 11 AM to 5 PM. Please check our store locator for specific timings.

How can I schedule a repair?

+

You can schedule a repair by calling our service center at (+230) 207 1700, visiting any Galaxy store, or filling out the service request form on our support page.

Do you have financing options?

+

Yes, we offer flexible financing options through various partners. Speak with our sales team at any Galaxy store to learn about available payment plans.
