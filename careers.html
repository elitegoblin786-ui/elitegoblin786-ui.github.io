Interested in joining our team?

TheBrandHouse is not just any company. It has strong values and aims to be the best in its sector as an employer, supplier and retailer, giving customers the best possible experience.

To achieve this, the company recruits and gives opportunities for staff development and, when a vacancy arises, seeks to recruit the best candidates. Appointments are made on merit.

Why join TheBrandHouse?

The company promotes growth, values commitment, and aims to build a workplace where high standards, strong service and long-term development go together.

Recruitment is based on merit, with opportunities for people who want to contribute, improve and grow within the business.

What kind of people do we look for?

  • Achievers with strong performance records
  • People who will show passion and commitment to their work
  • Individuals who believe in providing exemplary customer service
  • Employees who can embrace our corporate values and culture

Employee benefits

  • Incentives and rewards
  • Staff happiness program
  • Staff discount
  • Aid personal accident cover
  • Health scheme and company doctor
