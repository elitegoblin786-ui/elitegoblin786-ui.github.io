Distribution

JMG

JMG is the distribution arm of TheBrandHouse and is one of the most respected organisations in Mauritius in the field of home appliances and consumer electronics.

JMG acts as the essential link between brand owners and the market, importing, marketing and distributing a wide range of international brands.

View more

Formerly known as JM Goupille, which was incorporated in 1930, JMG imports, markets and distributes brands such as Asus, Beko, Black and Decker, Elba, Galanz, JBL, Panasonic, Samsung, Skyworth and Quest.

Product offering

  • Mobile devices: smart and feature phones, tablets, accessories and digital cameras
  • IT: laptops, screens and printers
  • Home appliances: fridges, freezers, washing machines, dish washers, ovens, cookers, built-in appliances and air conditioners
  • TV and audio-visual equipment
  • Small domestic appliances: microwaves and other household appliances

Market coverage

The sales and distribution teams cover Mauritius through retail networks, specialised resellers, mobile operators, hypermarkets, supermarkets and corporate customers including businesses, hotels, financial institutions and property developers.

Retailing

Service Centre

JMG Service Centre

The JMG Service Centre provides after-sales service for brands distributed by JMG.

The team delivers technical training either locally or overseas as provided by principals, and offers both indoor and outdoor repair facilities under and outside warranty.

View more

Qualified technicians undergo regular technical training, either locally or overseas, provided by principals. The service centre provides both indoor and outdoor repair facilities and under-warranty and out-of-warranty repairs.

Guarantees

Products distributed by JMG are covered by a minimum one year guarantee. A two year warranty applies to Samsung and Panasonic products, subject to conditions on the warranty card and date of purchase.

The service centre also guarantees the use of genuine spare parts sourced directly from principals.
