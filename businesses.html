Discover our businesses

TheBrandHouse operates through a connected platform of distribution, retail presence and accredited after-sales support.

Distribution

JMG

JMG is the distribution arm of TheBrandHouse and is one of the most respected organisations in Mauritius in the field of home appliances and consumer electronics.

JMG acts as the essential link between brand owners and the market, importing, marketing and distributing a wide range of international brands.

  • Mobile devices, tablets, accessories and digital cameras
  • IT products including laptops, screens and printers
  • Home appliances, built-in products and air conditioners
  • TV, audio-visual and small domestic appliances
Retailing

Galaxy and Samsung Brand Concepts

TheBrandHouse's retail arm trades through its Galaxy stores as well as three Samsung BrandStore locations.

Located throughout Mauritius, these stores are built to provide customers with high quality products, trusted advice and an excellent customer service experience.

  • Nationwide retail presence in urban and rural areas
  • Multi-brand shopping experience
  • Dedicated Samsung retail concepts in key malls
Service Centre

JMG Service Centre

The JMG Service Centre provides after-sales service for brands distributed by JMG.

The team delivers technical training either locally or overseas as provided by principals, and offers both indoor and outdoor repair facilities under and outside warranty.

  • Accredited service support for represented brands
  • Technical expertise and continuous training
  • Repair and customer care services for long-term brand trust
